The restaurants below are my 5 favorite restaurants!

  • Cowfish Sushi Burger Bar

    • Cowfish is an asian fusion restaurant located in Charlotte, NC. Their food is unique because dishes are a mix of asian and american style. My favorite dish is the Cowfish Bento Box.

  • Hot Spot

    • Hot Spot is another asian fusion restaurant that I am a huge fan of. It is located in Fairfax, VA. They bring broth, meats and other ingredients to the table. At that point, a communal experience begins and everyone eating cooks the food together.

  • Good Pie

    • Good Pie is a pizza spot in Las Vegas. It is located next to the pawn shop from the show Pawn Stars. They call themselves the "home of the grandma pizza." The pizzaiolo, Vincent Rotolo, is a great guy to talk to as well. Enjoy!

  • Futo Buta

    • Futo Buta serves Japanese cuisine and focuses on ramen dishes. It is located in Charlotte, NC. My favorite dish is the crispy rice squares served with tuna.

  • King Fish Poke

    • King Fish Poke is a poke restaurant that serves sushi bowls, burritos and salads. I enjoy the bowls the most - I always get tuna and sushi as my proteins. Try the King Fish Classic sauce when you try it out!
